Completos

Description

A completo is a Chilean-style hot dog (yes, there is a hot dog under there) in a fresh, soft bun that’s topped with diced onions, chopped tomatoes, ketchup, mustard, and mashed avocado. In Chile, they add lots of mayo to the mix but their mayonnaise is different than ours in the US, so we decided not to use that here.

Ingredients

  • hot dog buns / hoagie rolls
  • hot dogs – beef or pork
  • diced tomatoes
  • diced onions
  • smashed avocado
  • ketchup
  • mustard

Preparation

  1. Cook – The first thing you’ll want to do is boil or grill your hot dogs.
  2. Slice & Dice – While the hot dogs are cooking, you want to chop your onions and tomatoes, as well as mash-up your avocado.
  3. Layer – When you’re building your completo it’s all about layering in the proper order.
